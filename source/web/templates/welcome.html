Welcome to PinSheet

PinSheet helps you track and understand your golf stats without the noise. Enter rounds using the same shorthand you'd scribble on a scorecard, and get handicap index, trend analysis, and actionable insights — all stored locally on your computer with no accounts or network calls.

Start by adding a course, then enter your first round. PinSheet computes your handicap index using the WHS formula and tracks every aspect of your game across fairways, greens, putting, and short game.
